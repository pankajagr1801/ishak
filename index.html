Our Story

Our story began with our parents 'arranging' for us to meet and fall in love. And luckily for us they did because what followed from there has been a beautiful story which is translating into a lifetime of togetherness for us.


Deepak has always been a self reliant, independent person who is always looking out for his people. He loves to travel, watch movies, meet friends, learn new skills everyday. On the contrary Ishika is a big foodie, loves to travel and has a great sense of fashion. She treat everyone with respect and kindness, values friendship.
