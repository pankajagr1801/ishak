Our Story

Our story began with our parents 'arranging' for us to meet and fall in love. And luckily for us they did because what followed from there has been a beautiful story which is translating into a lifetime of togetherness for us.


Deepak has always been a self reliant, independent person who is always looking out for his people. He loves to travel, makes the most amazing food, has a great sense of humor and loves motor sports. On the contrary Ishika is a big foodie, loves to travel and is an adventure sports enthusiast.


While both of us are highly similar in nature, we are equally opposite and that is what makes us the best combination. We have discovered that we compliment and balance each other in the most beautiful manner.


We have had the most wonderful courtship filled with surprises and beautiful memories. We can't wait to start our lives together and look forward to celebrating the beginning of this on our wedding day with you all!
